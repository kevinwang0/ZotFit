What is ZotFit?

ZotFit is a revolutionary new health application that uses your data to develop a fitness regimen. Using artificial intelligence and machine learning, the engineers at ZotFit have developed a system that truly works with its users to help them attain their health-related goals.

How it works

First, our users sign up for an account and tell us what their goals are. They then export their health data from their phones and upload it to our servers here at ZotFit. After we do some magic and parse all that data, ZotFit recommends some routines that our users can do to help them achieve their goals. We also give users some additional “extra credit” homework (additional exercise videos and articles that may be relevant to them).

About the team

We are a collective of students at the University of California who wanted to help people who want to exercise get in shape. In developing ZotFit, we hope to improve the lives of people who have health related goals and empower them to get in shape.
